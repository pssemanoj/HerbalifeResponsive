HAP Activation Acknowledgement:

  • HAP is a monthly recurring order.
  • You can have two(2) HAP orders; one for personal use and another with products for resale;
  • Minimum 100 Volume Points/Maximum 1,000 Volume Points for personal use orders; and
  • Minimum discount 35%.
  • Each month on my HAP deadline date, Herbalife will automatically charge my credit card for the items on my HAP Standing Order.
  • Upon payment of my HAP Standing Order, it will be shipped within 4 business days after my monthly deadline date.
  • I may change my HAP order or cancel the HAP program at any time prior to or on my next deadline date on MyHerbalife.com in the HAP orders section or contact Member Services at 866-622-1222.
  • You must opt in to the HAP Program annually or your HAP Standing Order will be cancelled. 30 days prior to your HAP Standing Order expiration date, Herbalife will notify you to Opt in/ Opt out of the HAP program.
