ABOUT NET BANKING PAYMENTS:

Payment Processing

  • Minimum amount required for using Net Banking is Rs. 35/-INR.
  • You will see list of accepted bank on the gateway page.
  • Select a bank of your preference from the list and proceed to bank page.
  • You need to use your Personal Banking log in credentials to enter in to your account and follow further instructions as specified by the bank page.
  • In remarks you need to enter your associate ID.
  • Make the payment and complete the transaction.
  • On returning to Herbalife page unique reference number of the transaction should be displayed.
  • Your order will be processed once your transaction is successful.
  • End of Month – Net Banking transfer must be initiated on or before each country’s designated End of Month (EOM) day, and must be received and verified by the bank’s report by the 5th of the following month (regardless of what day the actual EOM falls on).
