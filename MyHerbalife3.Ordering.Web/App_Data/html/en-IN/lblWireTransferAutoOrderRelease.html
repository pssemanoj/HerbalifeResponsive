ABOUT ICICI I-SURE PAY PAYMENTS

    What is I-Sure Pay?

    1.Simpler way of order payment through ICICI bank to release order faster. Order gets released automatically on successful payment.

    How does it work?

    1. Place your orders on myherbalife.com or any of the sales Centres/Call Centre at 1800-102-2444 update your mobile number and update the payment mode as “ICICI Bank iSure Pay (Auto order release)”.
    2. SMS with Order number and amount will be sent to the mobile number as provided above.
    3. Pay the order amount as per the procedure defined below - “How to make the payment”.
    4. Depositing exact order amount against correct order reference will enable auto release of the order when the correct payment is received by Herbalife. SMS is sent on receipt of payment.
    5. In case of Pick-up order, reach-out to the QRC/ SC as mentioned at the time of placing the order, provide the order number and Pick-up the products
    6. In case of shipment order, shipment gets released faster

    How to make the payment?

    1. Amount can be paid through cash or through ICICI bank Cheque drawn on “Herbalife International India Pvt Ltd”. Please note that only ICICI Bank Cheques are accepted and Cheque would be accepted, only if funds are available in the account from which Cheque is drawn.
    2. Download the customized ICICI “I-Sure Pay” Pay-in Slip as provided in the below link.
    3. Update the details along with mandatory details of Associate ID and Order Number
    4. Update the details of ICICI Cheque in case of payment is through Cheque under “Cheque Deposit” provision or denominations in case of payment through cash.
    5. Handover the Pay-in Slip to teller at the ICICI bank counter along with cash or Cheque.
    6. On acceptance of the amount, SMS will be sent to the mobile number as mentioned at the time of placing the order, for receipt of amount and auto release of the order.
    7. In case of multiple orders, separate slips need to be filled and provided to the teller.
