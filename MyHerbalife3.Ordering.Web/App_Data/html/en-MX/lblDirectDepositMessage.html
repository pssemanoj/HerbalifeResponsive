ABOUT BENAVIDES PAYMENTS
  • Members may purchase products from Herbalife by Benavides transaction to complete payment for an order.
  • You must reference your Herbalife ID Number and order number with your Benavides transaction.
  • Please note that all orders must be paid in full for Herbalife to ship and release the order.
  • Benavides orders will be shipped when the correct payment is received by Herbalife.
