Credit Card Payment

  • Accepted cards: Visa and MasterCard only
  • Payment Processing: Upon completion of your transaction, do not close the payment gateway window. Once the transaction is successful, you will automatically be redirected back to Herbalife’s online ordering page.
    • Approved: An order confirmation number will be provided on the Herbalife Order Confirmation page.
    • Unsuccessful: if we are unable to obtain approval for the transaction, you will be directed back to the checkout page. Please kindly contact your issuing bank to obtain further details regarding transactions that were not successful.
