• Delivery days are from Monday to Saturday. Not available on Sundays & Public holidays.
  • Orders must be delivered within 10 working days from the date order is completed.
  • Orders completed after 2pm are available only for night slot on the next working day.
  • Orders completed after 5pm are available only from the third working day onwards.
  • Orders completed on Saturday and Sunday are available only from Tuesday onwards.

** The day when order is completed with successful payment is considered the first working day
** Subjected to slot availability, delivery will be based on first come first serve basis.
