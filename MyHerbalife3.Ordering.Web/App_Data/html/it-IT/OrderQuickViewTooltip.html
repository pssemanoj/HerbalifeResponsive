ORDER QUICK VIEW
Select, add, edit or delete the shipping or pick up location for your order.


Type
Indicates whether the order is for shipping or pick up.

Nickname
Frequently used shipping or pick up locations may be saved by nickname for ease of use. The next time you wish to use the same location, simply select it from the list of nicknames.


INVENTORY VIEW DESCRIPTION

Show All Inventory: When selected, all items are shown regardless of their availability.
Show Only Available Inventory: When selected, only items available for order for the specified shipping or pick up location are shown.

Inventory Legend Description
GREEN - Item is available for shipping or pick up orders.

YELLOW - Item is out of stock but may be ordered for shipment; once item is available it will be shipped. Items in this condition may not be added to orders for pick up.
RED - Item is out of stock and may not be added to pick up or shipping orders.
